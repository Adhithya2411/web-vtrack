About VTrack

VTrack is an innovative application designed for VIT students, faculty, and administrators to streamline access to results, attendance, and course-related information. It simplifies academic tracking, enhances transparency, and provides dedicated login portals for students, faculty, and administrators, ensuring a seamless and user-friendly experience for all stakeholders.

Features:

  • Real-time results tracking
  • Attendance monitoring
  • Course management
  • Personalized dashboards
  • Secure and reliable
